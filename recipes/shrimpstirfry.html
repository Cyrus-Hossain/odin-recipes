Shrimp Stir Fry

Description

The Shrimp Stir Fry recipe on Allrecipes is a 25-minute weeknight staple serving four, blending tender shrimp with frozen stir-fry veggies in a quick sesame-soy sauce thickened with cornstarch. Using simple ingredients like chicken stock, garlic, and 20 medium shrimp, it yields 169 calories per serving with solid protein and vitamin C—sauté veggies in sesame oil, add shrimp until pink, then simmer in sauce. Rated 4.6 stars by 57 reviewers, it's praised for ease and flavor, perfect over rice.
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen stir-fry vegetables
  • 20 uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix chicken stock, soy sauce, cornstarch, and garlic in a bowl; season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until oil shimmers; cook and stir vegetables in hot oil until softened, about 4 minutes.
  4. Add shrimp; cook and stir until shrimp begin to turn pink, about 3 minutes.
  5. Stir chicken stock mixture into vegetable-shrimp mixture. Continue to cook and stir until vegetables and shrimp are coated and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
