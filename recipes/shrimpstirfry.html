Shrimp Stir Fry

Description

The Shrimp Stir Fry recipe on Allrecipes is a 25-minute weeknight staple serving four, blending tender shrimp with frozen stir-fry veggies in a quick sesame-soy sauce thickened with cornstarch. Using simple ingredients like chicken stock, garlic, and 20 medium shrimp, it yields 169 calories per serving with solid protein and vitamin C—sauté veggies in sesame oil, add shrimp until pink, then simmer in sauce. Rated 4.6 stars by 57 reviewers, it's praised for ease and flavor, perfect over rice.
