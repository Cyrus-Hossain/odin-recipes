Unbelievable Chicken

Description

“Unbelievable Chicken” is a grilled-chicken recipe built around a bold, tangy marinade. Chicken breast halves are marinated for several hours in a blend of cider vinegar, coarse mustard, garlic, lime and lemon juice, brown sugar, salt, pepper, and olive oil, then grilled until just cooked through. The marinade delivers a sweet-tangy punch that seeps into the meat, producing juicy, flavorful chicken with a bright, zesty edge. Diners often remark on how the simple ingredients combine to make “everyone who tastes it asks me to share the recipe.”
  • 1/4 cup cider vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons prepared coarse-ground mustard or to taste
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar or to taste
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt or to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Mix cider vinegar, mustard, garlic, lime juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, salt, and pepper together in a large glass or ceramic bowl. Whisk in olive oil. Add chicken and toss evenly to coat. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator for 8 hours to overnight.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
  3. Lightly oil the grill grate. Place chicken on the preheated grill, and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, until juices run clear. Discard any remaining marinade.
